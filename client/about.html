About Threadle

Threadle is a social media platform that allows users to share and discuss posts on various topics. Our mission is to provide a platform for insightful conversation, where students can express their thoughts and ideas freely. We also aim to provide community to those who seek companionship on the web.

Threadle was created in 2024, and serves DLSU students as a web forum platform for students to speak their mind and hear from others.
